# Mushrooms on toast

Source: Sally

Tags: 

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 lbs friesh mushrooms
* 4 T olive oil
* 4 T chopped parsley
* 2 med cloves garlic
* salt/pepper

Saute the above over high heat for 5-8 minutes.

* 2 T butter
* 2 T lemon juice
* 1-2 T. wine vinegar
* (2 T cilantro)

Add the above to the mixture and saute another 5 minutes.

Serve over 

* 4-6 slices pumpernickle
